Some Do's and Don'ts for social media use-

Do’s:

Think before posting any content
2. Maintain a professional profile image and bio
3. Use privacy settings to control visibility
4. Verify information before sharing
5. Be respectful in comments and discussions

Don’ts:

Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
2. Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or controversial content
3. Do not click or share suspicious links
4. Avoid oversharing daily activities
5. Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
