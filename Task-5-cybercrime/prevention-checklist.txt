Stay Safe Online Checklist:

- Do not click on suspicious links in emails or messages
- Always verify the sender before sharing any personal information
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone
- Check website URLs carefully before entering login details
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions
- Regularly update your passwords

UPI Safety:
- Do not approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps
- Always double-check UPI ID or phone number before sending money

Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
